
<b>你幸福吗？8种永不改变的幸福感受</b>

Life has a way of constantly surprising us. Whether it's throwing us acurveball or shifting toward the better, our resilience is often tested. However, there are some happy constants we can always count on staying the same. These positive emotions will continue to light a spark in us, whether we're 18 or 80. Scroll through the list below of eight of our favorite happy feelings that will never change. What's a happy feeling that's a constant in your life?
生活总是处处充满着惊奇。有时它向我们投来一个弧线球，有时它也指引向上的道路，在这种种处境中不断的考验我们的自我修复力。然而，生活中也有些恒定的幸福，我们能确信它们不会改变。这些积极向上的情感总能将我们的内心点亮，无论我们是18岁还是80岁。看看下面这张列表，数数这8种永不会改变的幸福感受。你生活中恒常的幸福感受是什么？

1.The safe feeling of being with family.
和家人在一起的安全感。

2.How you feel after a really good nap.
美美的打了个盹儿后的感觉。

3.The freedom you feel on the first day it's warm enough to wear shorts.
天气转暖后，第一天可以穿短袖的自由感。

4.The unconditional love you get from a pet.
宠物给你的无条件的爱。

5.The nostalgic feeling triggered by the smell of a campfire.
篝火气味带来的返璞归真的幽思。

6.The sense of calm you feel when you hear the ocean lap the shore.
海浪拍击海岸带来的安宁感。

7.The joyful feeling of hearing little kids giggle.
听到孩子咯咯笑的愉悦。

8.How you feel after a belly laugh.
笑到肚子痛的感受。
